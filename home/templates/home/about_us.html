Our Story

Welcome to Silver Sands Estate, nestled in the heart of the picturesque McLaren Vale. At Silver Sands, we are passionate about crafting wines that reflect the rich diversity and unique terroir of Australia’s renowned wine region.

Founded with a dedication to quality, sustainability, and innovation, we have grown from humble beginnings into a celebrated winery offering a range of exquisite wines. Whether you prefer the bold complexity of our reds, the refreshing crispness of our whites, the elegance of our rosés, or the celebratory sparkle of our champagne, each bottle we produce carries the essence of the land and the art of winemaking.

Our journey is rooted in tradition but always looking ahead, blending time-honoured techniques with modern expertise to create wines that captivate the senses. At Silver Sands Estate, we believe that great wine starts in the vineyard, and we take pride in our commitment to sustainability and respect for the environment.

We invite you to join us in celebrating the beauty of nature, the joy of community, and the passion behind every bottle. From our vines to your glass, Silver Sands Estate offers a taste of McLaren Vale you’ll never forget.

Our Mission

Our mission is to craft exceptional wines that celebrate the beauty of nature and the art of winemaking. We are dedicated to sustainability, quality, and creating memorable experiences that connect people through the joy of wine.

Our History

Founded in 2010, Silver Sands Estate began as a passion project in the heart of the McLaren Vale. Over the years, we’ve grown from a small vineyard to an award-winning winery, known for our dedication to tradition, quality, and innovation in crafting wines that reflect the rich heritage of the region.

Our Founders

Founded by Emma and Lucas Harrison, lifelong wine enthusiasts with a shared passion for crafting exceptional wines. Their vision to blend tradition with innovation has shaped Silver Sands into a celebrated winery, reflecting the heart and soul of McLaren Vale.
